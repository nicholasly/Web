Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

There is something fresh and new about Lemon Meringue Pie. Strangely, there’s something nostalgic and lovely about it, too. This pie is sweet and perfect, just like the pie Grandma used to make…except for one major change. Instead of cooking your lemon custard in a finicky, boiling water bath (who keeps a double broiler on hand nowadays?), you get to thicken the filling with your trusty microwave. Old-fashioned flavors made with newfangled technology? Now, that’s a pie you can pull together in no time at all!
